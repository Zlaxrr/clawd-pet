There's a crab on your taskbar now.

Meet Clawd. He paces along the bottom of your screen, watches your cursor, naps when you wander off, and gets flung across the desktop when you grab him. Built in plain PowerShell and WinForms, so there is nothing to build and nothing to install.

The everyday, and the unexpected

Most of the time he is just being a crab. Sometimes he is up to something.

01

He walks the line

Real frame-by-frame leg animation pulled from the actual Clawd sprite, pacing the length of your taskbar.

02

Pick him up, throw him

He dangles from your cursor and swings on a string. Fling him and he arcs, bounces off the walls, and squashes on landing.

03

He rides your windows

Clawd climbs onto the top edge of your apps and rides along as you move them, then walks off when you close one.

04

A few secrets

Leave him alone and things happen. A balloon drifts down. The sky puts on a show. Something at the edge just… stares.

The part I am proudest of

He can read Claude Code's mind.

Turn on Claude Watch and a small bubble appears over his head telling you exactly what Claude is doing right now, complete with a little spark that beats while it works. When the task lands, you get a playful sign-off.

Getting him onto your screen

Windows 10 or 11. That's the only requirement.

  1. Download or clone the repo anywhere you like.
  2. Double-click start-clawd.vbs. First run grabs the official sprites; after that he just shows up.
  3. Want him watching Claude Code? Right-click tools\claude-watch-on.ps1 → Run with PowerShell.
PowerShell
# clone it, then wake him up
git clone https://github.com/Zlaxrr/clawd-pet
cd clawd-pet
# double-click start-clawd.vbs, or:
.\start-clawd.vbs

Clawd and all of his artwork belong to Anthropic. This is an independent fan project for personal use; the sprites are downloaded straight from claude.ai on first run and are never bundled here. The code is MIT.
